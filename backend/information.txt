App Name	Purpose
accounts	User authentication, JWT login/logout, roles (Admin, Manager, Cashier)
business	Business type setup and profile configuration (shop, hotel, etc.)
chartofaccounts	Manage the chart of accounts, account groups, and categories
transactions	Record income and expenses with multiple payment types
billing	Bill generation and management (includes PDF export)
reports	Financial reports (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
backup (optional in Phase 1)	Data backup/restore logic
common (shared utilities)	Common utilities like Nepali date formatting, constants, shared mixins
